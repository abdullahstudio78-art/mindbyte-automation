Privacy Policy - MindByte Publisher

Last updated: 2026-07-23

MindByte Publisher ("the app") is an internal automation tool used solely to publish video content that MindByte itself creates to its own TikTok account. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

What data we access

The app uses TikTok's Content Posting API to upload video files, titles, and captions to the MindByte TikTok account that has authorized this app. It does not access, collect, or store data belonging to any other TikTok user, and it does not read follower lists, direct messages, comments, or any personal information of viewers.

What we store

The app stores OAuth tokens (an access token and refresh token) required to publish videos on behalf of the MindByte account. These are stored as encrypted secrets in our automation provider (GitHub Actions secrets) and are never shared with, sold to, or accessed by any third party.

What we don't do

We do not use this app to access any TikTok account other than our own, do not run analytics on other users, and do not use collected data for advertising or resale.
